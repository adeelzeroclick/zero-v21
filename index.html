FAQs

        
Everything you need to know about Zero. Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch.

      

      

        
        
Zero is a search engine and activation layer for AI agents. It gives your coding agent instant access to thousands of real tools, APIs, and services — and handles payment per call — so it can do things it can't do on its own.

      

      

        
        
Install Zero once, then prompt your agent like normal. The moment it hits something it can't do — generate an image, place a call, deploy a site — Zero finds the right capability, calls it, and pays per use automatically. No keys, no setup, no context-switching.

      

      

        
        
Thousands of capabilities across media, communication, web & data, commerce, and infrastructure — generate images and video, place phone calls, scrape the web, charge a card, register a domain, deploy a website, and far more.

Zero pays per call in micro-USDC over open payment rails (x402 / MPP). You only pay for what your agent actually uses — down to fractions of a cent — with no subscriptions or commitments.
